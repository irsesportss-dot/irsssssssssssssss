Tournaments

To take part in the Tournament you need to click on the image of the Toornament to fill the Form.

Rules:

  • All payments must be made within the form itself. Any payments made outside the form will not be accepted.
  • Only online payments are allowed.
  • We aim to maintain a fair and transparent community, so every participant must be ready to share their screen upon request.
  • Refusal to share the screen will result in disqualification.
  • Rules for individual games are mentioned in the poster or image related to the specific tournament you wish to join.
  • You can click on the image to proceed and view more details.
